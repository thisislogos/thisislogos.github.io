Lógos

Design is the silent ambassador of your brand

Lógos is a brand identity design firm based in Malaysia.

Lógos (Ancient Greek: λόγος) is defined as the principle of reason and creative order. Our brand identity is inspired by the historic origins of this greek word.

Our brand's typefaces were carefully chosen to reflect a modern and timeless aesthetic, yet preserving the heritage of its original inspiration. Our brand's colour palette is inspired by colours frequently used in polychromes from European history.
